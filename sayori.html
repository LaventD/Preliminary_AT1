Hi there! I'm Sayori, the cheerful and bubbly member of the literature club in "Doki Doki Literature Club!" You'll usually find me with a smile on my face, ready to spread some sunshine and positivity wherever I go.

I may not be the most organized or put-together person in the world, but hey, that's part of my charm, right? Life's too short to worry about the little things, so I prefer to focus on enjoying each moment as it comes.

One of my favorite things to do is spend time with my friends in the literature club. Whether we're discussing our latest poems or just goofing off, being with them always brings a smile to my face. They're like a second family to me.

Sure, I may have my ups and downs, but who doesn't? I believe that it's okay to not be okay sometimes, and there's no shame in asking for help when you need it. After all, we're all in this crazy journey called life together, right?

So if you ever need someone to brighten your day or lend a listening ear, just know that I'm always here for you. Because at the end of the day, spreading happiness and making others smile is what I do best!

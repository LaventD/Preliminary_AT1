About Doki Doki Literature Club

Doki Doki Literature Club is a visual novel developed by Team Salvato. It is a psychological horror game that explores the darker side of interactive fiction.

The game is available for free on various platforms, including Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Gameplay

In Doki Doki Literature Club, players assume the role of a high school student who joins the school's literature club and interacts with its members. The gameplay primarily involves reading dialogues and making choices that affect the outcome of the story.

As players progress through the game, they uncover dark secrets about the club and its members, leading to multiple unexpected twists and turns.

Price

Doki Doki Literature Club is available for free on various digital distribution platforms, including Steam and the official website.

Features

  • Immersive storytelling
  • Multiple endings
  • Psychological horror elements
  • Interactivity and player choices
  • Engaging characters and narrative
